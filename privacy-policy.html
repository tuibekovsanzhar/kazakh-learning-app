1. Introduction

Qazaq Tili ("the App", "we", "us") is a Kazakh language learning application developed by Sanzhar Tuibekov. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us at tuibekov.sanzhar@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account, used solely for authentication.
  • Learning progress — your streak count, flashcard mastery marks, and quiz scores. This data is stored so your progress syncs across devices.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, payment information, or any other personal data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication — your email is used to create and manage your account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Progress sync — your learning data is saved to Firebase Firestore so you can pick up where you left off on any device.
  • Streak tracking — your daily streak is stored locally on your device and synced to the cloud.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than operating the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following Google/Firebase services, which have their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages sign-in with email and password.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your learning progress securely in the cloud.

You can review Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties for commercial purposes. Your data is used exclusively to operate the App.

6. Data Retention

Your account data (email address and learning progress) is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed from our systems.

7. Data Security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase infrastructure, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted by Firebase security rules — only you can read or write your own progress data.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at tuibekov.sanzhar@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.
